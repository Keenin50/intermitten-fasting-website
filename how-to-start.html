How to Start Intermittent Fasting

To get started with intermittent fasting, follow these steps:

  1. Choose your fasting window (e.g., 16 hours of fasting, 8 hours of eating).
  2. Gradually adjust your eating schedule.
  3. Stay hydrated during fasting periods.
  4. Eat healthy and balanced meals during eating windows.
